Mission Statement

The Training Zone website intends to increase the revenue of Training Zone (by 10%) by promoting the services offered by the company and increasing new and repeat customers.

Success Evaluation

The websites success will be determined by looking at the revenue generated by the Training Zone company in the three months leading to the release of the website and three months after the release of the website. if the total revenue has increased by 10% or more then the site will be considered successful

Target Audience

the target audience of the site will be local residents primarily between the ages of 28 and 50. since the site is aimed at an older audience attention will be payed to the contrasting colours to ensure that the site can be read by people who may have bad eye site.
